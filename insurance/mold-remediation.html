Emergency Services Insurance Guide

What's Typically Covered?

Mold remediation is covered only if caused by a covered peril like sudden water damage. Coverage limits often apply ($5,000-$10,000).

Common Exclusions

Mold from long-term leaks, humidity, condensation, or flood is excluded.

  • Gradual damage or wear and tear
  • Maintenance-related issues
  • Flood damage (requires separate flood insurance)
  • Earth movement (earthquake, landslide)
  • Intentional damage or neglect

How to File a Claim

  1. Document everything – Take photos/videos before any cleanup
  2. Mitigate further damage – Make temporary repairs (keep receipts)
  3. Contact your insurance company – File your claim immediately
  4. Meet with the adjuster – Point out all damage
  5. Get repair estimates – We work with insurance directly

Need Claim Assistance?

We work with all major insurance companies and can help with your claim.
