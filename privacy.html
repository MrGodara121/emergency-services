Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Request a quote or callback through our forms
  • Use our cost calculators
  • Submit questions or comments via email or contact forms
  • Sign up for newsletters or updates
  • Call the phone numbers listed on our site

This information may include your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, and details about your service needs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect you with appropriate service providers in your area
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your requests
  • For affiliate tracking and commission purposes
  • To analyze trends and usage of our site

Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Service Providers: When you request a quote or callback, we share your information with service providers in your area who can help with your needs.
  • Affiliate Partners: For tracking commissions when you purchase through affiliate links.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our website (analytics, hosting, email services).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Track affiliate referrals and commissions
  • Analyze site usage and improve user experience
  • Remember your preferences
  • Serve relevant content

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your information
  • The right to opt out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
