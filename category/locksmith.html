Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you arrive for lockout emergencies?
We guarantee 15-20 minute arrival time throughout all service areas. Our mobile units are strategically positioned to reach you fast. Call our 24/7 emergency number for immediate assistance.
How much does emergency locksmith service cost?
Car lockout: $50-150 depending on vehicle make/model. Home lockout: $75-200. After-hours service (after 8 PM or weekends) may add $50-100. We provide price estimate before dispatching – no hidden fees. Senior, military, and AAA discounts available.
Can you unlock my car without damaging it?
Yes, our trained technicians use specialized tools that unlock most vehicles without damage. We work on all makes and models, including luxury and newer vehicles with electronic locks. In rare cases where damage is unavoidable, we'll discuss options first.
Do you make car keys on the spot?
Yes, we can make most car keys on location including transponder keys and remote fobs. For newer vehicles with sophisticated electronics, we may need to program at your location which takes 15-30 minutes.
How often should I rekey my home?
Recommended when: You just moved in (always recommended), lost your keys, had a break-in, or had roommates/tenants move out. Rekeying is more affordable than replacing all locks. We can rekey most standard locks within 30 minutes.
