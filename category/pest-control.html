Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have termites?
Signs: mud tubes on foundation walls, hollow-sounding wood when tapped, discarded wings near windows/doors, small holes in drywall, termite droppings (frass) that look like sawdust. Annual inspections are recommended.
How much does termite treatment cost?
Treatment type varies: Liquid soil treatment: $4-12 per linear foot. Bait systems: $1,500-3,000 initial installation. Fumigation for whole house: $2,000-8,000. Free inspection to recommend the best approach for your situation.
How often should I get pest control service?
Quarterly treatments are recommended for general pest prevention. Monthly for active infestations. Termite bonds require annual inspections. Our plans are customizable to your needs.
Are your pest control treatments safe for pets and children?
Yes, we use EPA-approved products and follow all safety guidelines. We'll advise on when it's safe to re-enter treated areas. Eco-friendly and pet-safe options are available upon request.
What's the difference between termite bonds and treatments?
A termite bond is ongoing protection with annual inspections and retreatments if needed – like insurance. One-time treatment addresses existing infestation without ongoing coverage. We explain both options to help you decide.
