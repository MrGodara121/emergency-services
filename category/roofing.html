Insurance Information

Storm damage, hail, wind, and fallen trees typically covered by homeowners insurance. We work with Allstate, State Farm, Liberty Mutual, USAA, and Farmers. Free roof inspection with drone technology. Insurance claim specialists on staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my roof is damaged after a storm?
Check for: missing or damaged shingles, granules in gutters, leaks inside, dents on vents/flashing, or light visible from attic. Schedule a professional inspection â€“ we use drones for safe, thorough assessment without walking on your roof.
Does homeowners insurance cover roof replacement?
Typically yes if damage is from a covered peril like storm, hail, or fallen tree. Normal wear and tear is not covered. We work directly with insurance adjusters and can help with the claim process.
How long does a roof last?
Asphalt shingles: 20-25 years. Metal roofs: 40-70 years. Tile roofs: 50+ years. Wood shakes: 20-30 years. Regular inspections and maintenance extend lifespan.
What are signs I need roof replacement?
Multiple leaks, shingles curling or buckling, granules in gutters (lots), daylight through roof boards, sagging areas, or roof over 20 years old. Free inspection to assess condition.
How much does roof replacement cost?
Costs vary: Asphalt shingle: $5-8 per sq ft. Metal: $8-15 per sq ft. Tile: $10-20 per sq ft. Includes tear-off, underlayment, installation, and cleanup. Size, pitch, and accessibility affect final price. Free estimate provided.
